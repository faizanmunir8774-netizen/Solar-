About The Project

Intelligent Solar Energy Harvesting

Our automatic solar tracker maximizes energy capture by continuously orienting panels toward the sun using real-time sensor feedback and servo-driven mechanics.

Problem Statement

Fixed solar panels lose up to 40% of potential energy due to suboptimal sun angles. Manual adjustment is impractical and inefficient for continuous operation.

Our Solution

A dual-axis automated tracking system using LDR sensors and microcontroller-driven servos to maintain optimal panel orientation throughout the day. Our system achieves real-time sun tracking with precision and minimal power consumption.

Energy Optimization

Increases solar energy capture efficiency by tracking the sun's path across the sky, maximizing power generation throughout the day.

Smart Control System

Microcontroller-based automation with adaptive algorithms that adjust tracking parameters based on environmental conditions.

Performance Monitoring

Real-time data logging and analysis capabilities for tracking system performance and energy generation metrics.
